EDUCATIONAL RESOURCES

In the Philippines, Voting Involves These Steps:

Registration: Eligible citizens must register with Comelec by providing ID and personal details.

Election Day: Voters go to assigned precincts after checking voter info for precinct number and location.

Verification: Present valid ID at the precinct for verification and receive the official ballot.

Marking the Ballot: Voters mark choices clearly on the ballot provided, following instructions.

Casting the Vote: After marking, voters insert the ballot into the designated box or machine.

Verification and Receipt: Some systems provide verification or receipts for vote confirmation.

Note: The process may vary based on the election and Comelec updates. It's crucial to follow Comelec's guidelines for each election. Participating in voting is a vital civic duty!
