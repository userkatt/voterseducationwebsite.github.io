About Us

This website is all about the following:

1. Empowering Philippine Citizens For Responsible Voting:
This website emphasizes the importance of voter education in empowering Philippine citizens to make informed decisions at the polls and fostering responsible voting behavior.
2. Making Voter Education a Pillar of Democracy in the Philippines:
This document highlights the significance of voter education in producing a mature and informed electorate, contributing to the overall democratic process in the Philippines.
3. Power to the people: An in-depth look on voter education:
This article discusses the importance of voter education in preparing the Filipino electorate for national elections and emphasizes the need for effective voter education programs.
4. Civic education and the Filipino voter:
This opinion piece highlights the role of civic society groups in promoting voter registration and implementing voter education programs in preparation for the upcoming elections in the Philippines.
